About Angela

Healing From the Heart

Welcome. I'm Angela, the founder of Chiyu Reiki & Natural Wellness. The word Chiyu (癒) is Japanese for healing - and it lies at the heart of everything I do.

Reiki is a beautiful, gentle Japanese healing technique that works with the body's own energy to promote deep relaxation, balance and healing. Whether you're navigating stress, chronic pain, emotional challenges, or simply seeking stillness, my sessions offer a compassionate, nurturing space for you.

Based in the beautiful coastal town of Hythe, I offer in-person and distance healing sessions tailored entirely to you.
